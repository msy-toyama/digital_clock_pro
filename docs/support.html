Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí scroll down and tap "Restore Purchase". Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

How do I make the clock full screen?

Simply tap anywhere on the clock display. Tap again to exit full-screen mode. In full-screen mode, all UI elements are hidden for a clean, distraction-free display.

How do I switch between clock designs?

Long-press on the clock display to open the design gallery. You can browse through all available themes and tap to select one.

How do I create my own custom watch design?

Tap the "Create" tab at the bottom of the screen. You can customize fonts, colors, backgrounds, and more. When you're happy with your design, tap "Save" to add it to your collection.

Why can I only save one custom design (Free version)?

The free version includes 1 custom watch slot to let you experience the feature. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited custom watch saves and access to all 100+ design themes.

Is Premium a subscription?

No! Premium is a one-time purchase. Pay once and enjoy all Premium features forever‚Äîno recurring charges.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings ‚Üí tap on your current language ‚Üí select your preferred language from the list. The app supports 27 languages.

Does the clock work when the screen is locked?

The app displays time while it's open. For always-on display, enable your device's Always-On Display feature if available (iPhone 14 Pro and later).

How accurate is the time?

Digital Clock Pro uses your device's system time, which is automatically synchronized with Apple's time servers. The time should be accurate to within a second.

Can I use the timer in the background?

Yes! Start a timer and you'll receive a notification when it completes, even if you switch to another app or lock your device.
